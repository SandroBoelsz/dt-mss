Welcome to the Digital Twin Migration Support System

This tool guides you through the process of analyzing and decomposing your existing Digital Twin into reusable problem-solving components within a Virtual Research Environment.

  • Guided through five phases: Viewpoints Analysis, Correspondence, Modularity Level (Code & Non-code), Component Decomposition, and Metadata Enhancement
  • Step-by-step decomposition of monolithic, coarse-grained, and fine-grained components
  • Interactive tables to edit and keep track of intermediate results
  • Export your work as a downloadable JSON file containing all component metadata, ready for documentation, sharing, or further integration in your VRE workflow
